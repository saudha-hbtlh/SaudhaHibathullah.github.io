Situation:

You have $10,000 to spend on advertisements, but you don't know how to distribute it across social platforms. Without data, you risk wasting budget on low-performing campaigns. That's where Adventa comes in — an AI-powered tool that forecasts revenue before you spend a cent.

Task:

Build a complete, one‑click solution that:
✅ Predicts revenue for each platform and campaign
✅ Shows ROI percentage and budget reallocation recommendations
✅ Handles large CSV files (up to 200MB)
✅ Displays intuitive charts, heatmaps, and plain‑language explanations

Action:

Adventa was built in 7 days using a lean data‑science stack:

📁 Data Ingestion: Drag‑and‑drop or upload CSV files up to 200MB. The app automatically detects columns and prepares data for training.

🧠 Machine Learning: A scikit‑learn pipeline (Linear Regression / Random Forest) is trained with one click. The model predicts revenue based on ad spend, impressions, clicks, and platform features.

📈 Real‑time Insights: The dashboard shows actual vs predicted revenue, feature importance heatmaps, and key metrics like R², MAE, and ad stock effect.

💰 Budget Reallocation: Adventa recommends exactly how to reallocate your $10,000 budget across platforms to maximize ROI, with a clear positive/negative message.

🖥️ Full Dashboard: Four analytical views — overview, model performance, budget optimizer, and data explorer.

Result:

✅ Predict revenue before spending — up to 92% accuracy on test data
✅ Instant ROI percentage and actionable recommendations
✅ Full transparency with actual vs predicted graphs
✅ Budget reallocation in seconds, not days
✅ Deployed on Streamlit Cloud — accessible from any device
